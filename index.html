Interacting with Models

If you are using a mouse & keyboard, the WASD keys will move the camera around and your mouse buttons will manipulate the 3D models. When you hover over an object you will see tool tips like this:

If you are using a VR headset, you will need controllers:

  • Left Thumbstick = Move around the environment.
  • Right Thumbstick = Turn camera.
  • Left Trigger = Clicking on buttons and models.
  • Right Trigger = Pick up and move a model.
  • Right Trigger + Grip = Rotate Model
